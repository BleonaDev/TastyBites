Chocolate Cake 🎂
Ingredients:
  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 200g sugar
  • 100g cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 100ml milk
  • 100g butter (melted)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
Instructions:

Preheat oven to 180°C. Mix flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt in one bowl. In another bowl, whisk eggs, sugar, milk, butter, and vanilla. Combine mixtures, pour into a greased pan, and bake for 30–35 minutes until cooked through.

Enjoy your delicious chocolate cake! 😋

Homemade Pizza 🍕
Ingredients:
  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 150ml warm water
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 100g mozzarella cheese
  • 100g tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves
Instructions:

Mix flour, yeast, salt, water, and olive oil to form a dough. Let rise for 1 hour. Roll out, spread tomato sauce, top with mozzarella and basil, then bake at 220°C for 12–15 minutes until golden and bubbly.

Enjoy your homemade pizza! 😋

Chicken Avocado Burrito 🌯
Ingredients:
  • 2 large tortillas
  • 200g cooked chicken breast
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 50g shredded cheese
  • ½ cup cooked rice (optional)
  • ½ cup black beans (optional)
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 tbsp sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • Fresh coriander (optional)
  • Salt & pepper
Instructions:

Warm the tortillas. Slice the chicken and avocado. Layer chicken, avocado, beans, rice, tomato, cheese, and sour cream on each tortilla. Season with salt and pepper, add coriander if desired. Roll into a burrito, tucking the sides in. Serve warm!

Pan Fried Steak 🥩
Ingredients:
  • 2 beef steaks (sirloin or ribeye)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • Fresh rosemary or thyme (optional)
  • Salt & freshly ground black pepper
Instructions:

Season steaks generously with salt and pepper. Heat oil or butter in a pan over high heat. Add garlic and herbs, then place steaks in the pan. Cook 2–4 minutes per side depending on doneness preference. Rest the steaks for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy with your favorite sides!

Cheesy Pasta 🍝
Ingredients:
  • 200g pasta
  • 100g grated cheddar cheese
  • 50ml heavy cream
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Instructions:

Boil the pasta, then prepare a garlic-butter sauce with cream and melted cheese. Toss the pasta in the sauce until well coated, season with salt and pepper, and serve hot, garnished with parsley if desired.

Enjoy your creamy, cheesy pasta! 😋

California Sushi
Ingredients:
  • Sushi rice
  • Nori sheets
  • Crab sticks
  • Avocado slices
  • Cucumber strips
  • Soy sauce & wasabi
Instructions:

Lay nori on a bamboo mat, spread sushi rice evenly, add crab, avocado, and cucumber, then roll tightly. Slice and serve with soy sauce and wasabi. 🍣

Enjoy your California sushi! 🍣

Roasted Salmon 🍣
Ingredients:
  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Fresh herbs (thyme or dill)
Instructions:

Preheat oven to 200°C. Place salmon on a baking tray, drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and top with lemon slices and herbs. Roast for 12–15 minutes until cooked through.

Enjoy your hot roasted salmon! 😋

Creamy Risotto 🍚
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Instructions:

Sauté onion in butter until soft, add rice and toast for 1-2 minutes. Gradually add warm broth, stirring constantly, until rice is creamy and cooked. Stir in Parmesan, season, and serve hot.

Enjoy your creamy risotto! 😋

Garlic Butter Shrimps 🍤
Ingredients:
  • 300g shrimps
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt & pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
Instructions:

Melt butter in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant. Add shrimps, season with salt and pepper, cook until pink and cooked through. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.

Baked Rainbow Trout 🐟
Ingredients:
  • 2 rainbow trout fillets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • Lemon slices
  • Fresh herbs (thyme/dill)
Instructions:

Preheat oven to 200°C. Place trout on a baking tray, drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, top with lemon slices and herbs. Bake for 12-15 minutes until cooked through. Serve warm.

Ramen Noodles 🍜
Ingredients:
  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 boiled egg
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Soy sauce to taste
Instructions:

Cook noodles according to package instructions. Heat broth and add noodles. Top with boiled egg, green onions, and drizzle with soy sauce. Serve hot.

Strawberry Cheesecake 🍰
Ingredients:
  • 200g cream cheese
  • 50g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 100g digestive biscuits, crushed
  • 50g butter, melted
  • Strawberries for topping
Instructions:

Mix crushed biscuits with melted butter, press into a pan for base. Whisk cream cheese, sugar, and egg until smooth, pour over base. Bake at 160°C for 25 minutes. Chill, top with strawberries, and serve.
